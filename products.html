Product layers

Qincept is built as layered infrastructure.

The platform is being developed as a structured system for communication, cases, governance, institutional memory, and future AI-assisted operations.

Institutional Memory Layer

Events, decisions, ownership trails, case history, escalation records, and searchable continuity.

Governance Layer

Audit trails, permissions, role accountability, approval context, and evidence-based workflows.

Operations Layer

Cases, assignments, due dates, follow-ups, department coordination, and unresolved issue tracking.

AI Context Layer

Structured institutional data that can support summaries, risk signals, recommendations, and future agents.
